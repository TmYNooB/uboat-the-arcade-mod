Transform UBOAT into a high-octane arcade experience inspired by classic U-Boat sims like 'Das Boot'. This mod strips away the complexity and brings pure submarine action to the forefront.

[h2]⚠️ Note[/h2]

This is a total arcade conversion. Expect unlimited resources, instant repairs, and pure combat focus. The sim stays home—this is pure submarine action.

[h3]Known Issue: AA Weapon Balance[/h3]
Currently, small-calibre AA guns (20mm and above) significantly outperform deck guns in direct ship combat. This is being evaluated and may be rebalanced in a future update to restore deck guns as the primary anti-ship weapon. For now, expect AA weapons to be highly effective against surface targets.

[h2]🗑️ After Updating – Clear Game Cache[/h2]

If the mod doesn't seem to take effect after an update, you need to [b]manually delete the game's temporary folders[/b]. UBOAT caches processed data and won't pick up mod changes until the cache is cleared. Do this after each update.

Delete (or empty) the following folders — they are recreated automatically on next launch:
[list]
[*][code]%UserProfile%\AppData\LocalLow\Deep Water Studio\UBOAT\Temp[/code]
[*][code]%UserProfile%\AppData\LocalLow\Deep Water Studio\UBOAT\Cache[/code]
[*][code]%UserProfile%\AppData\LocalLow\Deep Water Studio\UBOAT\Data Sheets[/code]
[/list]
[b]How to get there quickly:[/b] Press [b]Win + R[/b], paste the path (replace [b]%UserProfile%[/b] with your Windows username or just type it as-is), press Enter.
